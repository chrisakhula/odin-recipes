Kachumbari

Kachumbari takes the accolade of most delicious salad of Kenya. Trust me when I say kachumbari is the epitome of simplicity. It is so simple you can whip it up in no time.

Serve Kachumbari as a side dish or condiment for pilau rice, kenyan grilled meat known as "Nyama Choma" or even Mutura.

Ingredients

  • Fresh ripe tomatoes
  • onions
  • green/red pepper
  • lemon juice
  • coriander
  • Avocado

Method

  1. Wash all the vegetables required for making Kachumbari then pat them dry with a disposable kitchen towel.
  2. Chop the onions finely, add them in a bowl of salted water and wash for about a minute. This process eliminates the pungent and bitter taste present in the onions. It also makes the milder and more flavorful.
  3. Chop all other ingredients and add them in a large bowl
  4. Add the lime juice, olive oil and salt to taste and you're good to go.
